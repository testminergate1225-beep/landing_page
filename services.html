Our Services

We offer a range of evidence-based CBT services tailored to your needs — from individual therapy to specialist interventions.

🧠 Individual CBT Therapy

One-to-one sessions with a trained therapist. Tailored to your specific issues and goals, focusing on identifying and changing negative thought patterns.

✅ Most common and personalised form of CBT

👥 Group CBT Sessions

Therapy conducted in small groups. Learn alongside others facing similar challenges and benefit from peer support.

✅ Often used for anxiety, depression, or social skills

💻 Online / Remote CBT

Sessions via video calls, phone, or apps — flexible and accessible from home for convenience and privacy.

✅ Very popular for convenience and privacy

📞 Initial Consultation / Assessment

First session to understand your concerns. The therapist evaluates your needs and suitability for CBT to create a personalised plan.

✅ Helps create a personalised treatment plan

🎯 CBT for Specific Conditions

Specialised therapy for anxiety disorders, depression, OCD, PTSD, phobias, stress & burnout, and more.

✅ Focused and structured treatment plans

🛠️ Behavioural Therapy Interventions

Practical strategies like exposure therapy, habit reversal and activity scheduling to translate thinking changes into action.

✅ Helps translate thinking changes into action

🧾 Guided Self-Help CBT

Workbooks, exercises or digital programs supported by a therapist for a lower-cost, flexible option.

✅ Lower cost and flexible option

👨‍👩‍👧 Couples or Family CBT

Improve communication and relationships, addressing shared patterns of thinking and behaviour.

✅ Useful for conflict resolution and emotional support

🧘 Stress Management & Resilience Training

Techniques to handle pressure, including relaxation, mindfulness and coping skills — often offered as short courses.

✅ Often offered as short courses or workshops

📚 Psychoeducation

Teaching clients about how thoughts and emotions work to empower long-term self-management.

✅ Helps empower long-term self-management
